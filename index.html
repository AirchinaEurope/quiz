Explore the New Gateway:
Air China Launches Frankfurt to Beijing Daxing Flights

Enhancing connectivity on one of the world's busiest intercontinental corridors

As the largest carrier on China-Europe routes, Air China operates 37 routes offering nearly 70 daily flights. Benefiting from the deepened joint venture with Lufthansa Group, Air China seamlessly connects corporate passengers with shared premium lounge access at Frankfurt Airport.

The newly launched daily Frankfurt–Beijing Daxing (PKX) service utilizes Boeing 777 aircraft featuring a First Class cabin. This new gateway optimizes travel to the Beijing-Tianjin-Hebei region and Xiong'an New Area via China's premier integrated transport hub. This expansion of the route network offers travelers significantly greater flexibility in planning their business trips.
